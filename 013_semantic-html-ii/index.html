Who are 100 Thieves and How much are they Worth?

Who are 100 Thieves?

A lifestyle brand and gaming organization based in Los Angeles. Reprsented by elite pro gamers in different video games such as Call of Duty, League of Legends, Valorant, and Fortnite. Their League of Legends team is part of the Leage of Legends Champsionship series in North America ran by Riot Games. The Thieves' League of Legends team is a League of Legends Championship Series franchise team, the top-level esports league in the US and Canada run by Riot Games. Sponsored by Chipotle, Twisted Tea, Truly, Stock X, JBL, AT&T, Cash App and Rocket Mortgage.

How much is 100 Thieves Worth?

As of 2021, they are among the top five most valuable Esports companies, with an estimated $190 million net worth. Has estimated revenue of $16 million

Titles and Championships Won

At CWL (Call of Duty World League) London 2019, is when the team won the organization's first ever championship title.

Very recently, the 100 Thieves League of Legends Esports team won the 2021 Summer LCS (League of Legends Championship Series) title, their first in the league.
